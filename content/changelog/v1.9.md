## v1.9 — Invoice Generation, Client Billing & Work Tracking

The biggest release yet — transforming Budget Planner from a personal finance tracker into a complete financial tool for freelancers and solo operators.

### The Closed Loop

The killer feature is the end-to-end workflow: **log your work -> generate an invoice -> mark as paid -> income flows into your budget**. No other tool connects client billing directly to personal budgeting.

### What Changed

This release adds 3 new modules (Invoice, Client, Work Entry), redesigns the dashboard, restructures the sidebar, fixes recurring transactions, and adds a data reset feature. Over 50 commits built by the Council of Agents.
