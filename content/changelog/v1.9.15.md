version: v1.9.15
date: 2026-05-23
title: Correct Currency Symbols and Branded Invoice PDFs
status: current
description: >
  Invoice totals now render with the right currency symbol in every supported
  currency, fixing a real correctness bug where the peso, won, and baht signs
  collided with the first digit and made amounts unreadable. Your invoices can
  now carry your business identity: set a business name, address, Tax ID, and
  payment instructions, and the PDF leads with your name and tells clients
  exactly how to pay. The whole invoice PDF got a redesign to match, and the
  changelog no longer shows two "Latest" badges at once.
features:
  - title: Currency Symbols Fixed
    items:
      - "**Totals are now unambiguous in every currency.** The PDF font had no glyph for the peso (₱), won (₩), or baht (฿) signs, so the symbol crashed into the first digit: ₱4,706.80 read as *₱,706.80* and ₱85 looked like *฿5*. Every supported currency symbol now renders cleanly, with a clear gap before the amount."
      - "This hit the Philippine peso hardest, but the fix is universal. The Japanese yen was also normalized to the standard ¥ so it matches the rest of the app."
      - "No action needed: open any existing invoice and the totals now read correctly."
  - title: Business Identity on Your Invoices
    items:
      - "New **Business & Invoice Details** section in your profile lets you set a business name, address, Tax ID, and payment instructions once and have them flow onto every invoice you send."
      - "When set, your invoice PDF leads with your business name as the masthead and shows a full sender block: name, email, phone, address, and Tax ID, so clients know exactly who the invoice is from."
      - "A dedicated **How to Pay** block carries your payment instructions, so clients see how to pay without asking. Set it once in your profile and it appears on every invoice."
      - "Haven't filled these in yet? Invoices fall back gracefully to the previous clean layout, so nothing breaks until you're ready."
  - title: Redesigned Invoice PDF
    items:
      - "**A cleaner, more professional invoice.** Your business name now sits at the top as a wordmark masthead, with a richer From block beneath it."
      - "The **How to Pay** panel fills the space opposite the totals that used to sit empty, balancing the layout and giving payment details a home."
      - "Line items are laid out as a crisp data table with thin rules, the invoice number now appears in the footer, and the DRAFT watermark is consistently centered."
  - title: Fixes
    items:
      - "**Smoother dashboard card carousel.** The account cards now shuffle continuously when they rotate: the next card slides up as the current one is dealt off the top, replacing the brief freeze where a card used to fade out before the next one appeared."
      - "The changelog no longer shows two *Latest* badges at once. Two versions were both marked current; now only the newest carries the Latest badge."
